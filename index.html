Our Services

Full Grooming

We offer a full grooming service for your exotic animal. This includes a bath, haircut, nail trim, and ear cleaning. Your pet will leave feeling clean and refreshed.

Bath and Brush

If your pet is not in need of a haircut, we offer a bath and brush service. This includes a bath, brush, nail trim, and ear cleaning. Your pet will leave looking and feeling great.

Nail Trim

If your pet is in need of just a nail trim, we offer this service as well. We will trim your pet's nails safely and efficiently.
